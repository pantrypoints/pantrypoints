## 1. No Data Collection
Pantrypoints Farm ("the App") is designed with your privacy as a priority. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information or user data.

The App operates entirely on your device and does not:
- Collect personal information (name, email, phone number, address, etc.)
- Track your location
- Access your contacts, photos, or files
- Collect device information (IMEI, MAC address, serial numbers, etc.)
- Use cookies or tracking technologies
- Share any data with third parties
- Store any information on external servers


## 2. Local Storage Only
All information you enter into the App is stored exclusively on your device using local storage:

- Plot names and locations
- Crop selections
- Planting dates
- Growth notes

This data never leaves your device and is only accessible by you.

## 3. No Internet Required for core functionality

The App functions primarily offline. No internet connection is required for core functionality, and the App does not transmit any data over the internet.

## 4. No Analytics or Crash Reporting

The App does not use:
- Analytics tools (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.)
- Crash reporting services
- Performance monitoring tools
- Any other data collection SDKs

## 5. No Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with:
- Social media platforms
- Advertising networks
- Cloud storage services
- Any external APIs or services

## 6. Permissions

The App may request minimal permissions only for features you explicitly use:

- Notifications (optional): Only if you enable reminders for plant care
- Storage (Android only): Only for exporting/importing your farm data if you choose to backup

These permissions are never used to collect data and can be denied without affecting core functionality.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted within the App. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
