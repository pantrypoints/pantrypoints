We put up a prototype of our Galaxiv game here: https://galaxiv.pantrypoints.com/game


![Galaxiv](/graphics/galaxiv.jpg)

Galaxiv is supposed to make people familiar with stars beyond the sun. This is because Supereconomics (the science behind Pantrypoints) allows interplanetery and interstellar trade. 

The game makes players mindful of the balance between:
- population size (1st law of value)
- resource efficiency (2nd law of value)
- resources available (3rd law of value)
- economic expansion (4th law of value)
