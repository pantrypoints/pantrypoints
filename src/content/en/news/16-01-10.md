We got accepted into the NASA Space apps challenge in Saigon! 

![NASA Space Apps](/covers/nasa.jpg)


Our entry is [NARA or Non-Arbitrary Resource Allocation](https://2016.spaceappschallenge.org/challenges/earth/emobile-pastoralism/projects/social-resource-allocation-sora):

It focuses on pastoralism for African countries like Malawi. We deployed it on Heroku.
